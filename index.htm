Dr. Mathew Vairamon, Esq.


Dr. Mathew Vairamon also known as Mathai Vairamon Mathew is an Attorney at Law from the state of Louisiana. I am licensed as an attorney in the state of Louisiana in 2010, Southern Federal court in Houston, Texas in 2011 and United States Tax Court in 2011. About 15 years, I was an Advocate in India. I started my practice in Delhi and later moved to Kollam, Kerala, India.

I taught as an Adjunct Professor in Texas Southern University (TSU), Houston, Texas from 2011 to 2012. I also taught as an Adjunct Instructor in Prairie View A & M University in Prairie View, Texas from spring 2014 to spring 2018. Besides my teaching, I was a faculty advisor to students from 2014 to 2017 in Prairie View A & M University. Currently, I am a Lecturer in Prairie View A & M University.

I practice as an Attorney from 2010 onwards in Houston, Texas. My major areas of practice are immigration laws, both family and business immigration laws, IRS exemption for non-profit organization, social security laws, preparing legal documents etc.

I am a notary in the state of Texas and an accredited general and family mediator from Harris County DRC, Houston, Texas.

I got my Ph.D in Administration of Justice from Texas Southern University, Houston, Texas in 2013 and M.S degree in Criminal Justice from University of Houston-Downtown in 2005. My name was included in the National Dean’s list in 2006 after my MS degree. Also, I have LL.B degree from Bombay University, India and LL.M degree with second rank from Mahatma Gandhi University, Kottayam, Kerala, India. I completed my education in Certified Financial Planner course (CFP) from University of Texas (UT), San Antonio, Texas in 2014.

I have published several books and CDs in Malayalam and articles in Malayalam and English. Some of my English articles were included in academic book, journal and Encyclopedia.
